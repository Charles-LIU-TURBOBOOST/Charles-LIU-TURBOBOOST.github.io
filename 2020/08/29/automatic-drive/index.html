automatic drive 1

发表于 2020-08-29

​ Automatic Drive is one of the most outstanding conjectures for humanbeings. For several decade, thousands of sectors like Goole and Microsoft are chasing for completing the actual car that can help the drive to achieve the unmanned drive.

Recently, I have participated an event which aim to help student to achieve the lowest level of unmanned drive that use different to highlight the way that small model car need to go.
There are 3 main steps that must be completed to ensure that the model car is able to go and each step has corresponding software.

​ First things first, a 3-D printer is essentail for this process in order to print out the comopents of the car in which needs solid works( A software ) to help build the 3-D model in the computer and save them as binary file to print them out. Moreover, the 3-D printer is also absolutely fascinating. For people who learn about the Integration(definite integral), they must learns about the priciple which the area under the curve, is the sum of areas of infinite small rectangles with extremly small width multiply its corresponding height.

3-D print use the similar priciple, it dose not print the model in just one time but in fact, the model is made up of many small printed lines. The printer first print the frame of model and then use the plastic lines to fill the frame up.

​ Secondly, when the car model is printed successfully and the electric machineries are combined, an aduino chip is needed to control the movement of the wheels. The language that used on wrting code in

aduino chip is just C which I have learnt before the start of event. So it is not very difficult stuff for me.

Thirdly, a camera is needed to locate on the top of the car to transfer the signal to the aduino chip to control the speed when the camera scans different colors.The language that used in OPEN MV (to control the camera) is python and I am not familiar with this language, a little of help from teacher is essential and I

am sure that I will learn this in this year
