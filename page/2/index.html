Conclusion and Expectation

发表于 2020-01-04 | 分类于 Experience-of-Study

#Introduction

​ Hey guys, this is my first article that I actually write about myself. My name is Liukai (Charles for my English name) Today’s topic is about my conclusion for my second year of my high school life. Actually I my class is a little bit different from the normal high school students in China , I am now studying in the international department in Jingling High School . In todays article I would like to talk about 2 main points which conclusion and expectation respectively.

  • Conclusion

    The winter holiday is now coming , the first of second year will end in the next few weeks. Actually , I have just finished my end-term examinations， including Pure maths , physics, economics and chemistry. In fact , chemistry and physics are my weakness while the other two subjects are my strengths. As a student who decide to study abroad , excellent academic performance is significantly important compared with other characteristics that a freshman who study overseas. Working and dealing things by myself is also essential for me to learn, what I have expected now is that doing some voluntary jobs in the winter holiday during the Spring Festival . Being as a volunteer , I can develop the skills of communication.

  • #Expectations

Apart from this ，it is now very significant for me to decide which country I need to go when I graduate from my school ，till now ，my first choice is UK and the second choice is just the Canada ( That are what usually my classmates choose) .In fact , I do decide to choose a major which is relevant to the economics ( profitable and not brain-consuming for me ),but I have found that science subjects are my cups of tea which what I am interested in . So in conclusion , science subjects like maths are what I am keen on. The marks of my final exam are coming soon. Good luck , Charles you can make it！
