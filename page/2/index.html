how to adopt a personal statement

发表于 2020-04-13 | 分类于 Experience-of-Study

Today I would like to talk about how to edit your PS (personal statement)

The steps are very simple. Just send your PS to your English teachers and they will help you to change the mistakes and improve your grammar.

So, is that the only method that can help you adjust your PS

Obviously , the answer is NOT

There several tips that can help you (including me) to improve our PS

The first one is just the get the help from the software (microsoft word ). Change the language mode into the British English mode which can automatically find you spelling mistakes and some of the grammar mistakes . From my first draft that I wrote , I did find some of the spelling mistakes that marked by red colour by the software and format mistake marked by the blue colour

The second one is make a plan of what you are going to write before going practicing. Honestly , in my first draft that sent to my teacher , there were lots of sentence that were not very logical which means there were no clearly connection between former sentence and next sentence. Thus , the teacher could not understand the meaning clearly . So, make sure you have already read your PS , microsoft can not help you because it is not grammar or spelling mistakes .

How to write a personal statement for university entry

Introduction: why we need to write PS

PS is an article that can help the candidate to show their characteristics and studying skills to the university.

What we need to write in the PS ? and what is the most important part ?

In general , we need to write different parts in our PS and from my perspective , academic performance , expectation and activities are 3 essential and activity part is the most significant component .

What we need to write in our activity part

Actually , it is not so difficult once we know how to relate our afterschool activities to our ideal major or courses. Andrew , our PS teacher who from the UK tells us that we need to write down what we have done in high school life (a story that related to computer if your major is EE or programming)

Let us take a wrong example , one of friends named XXX has written his first draft of PS and his ideal major is environmental science . In his paragraph , he wrote much about the geography knowledge that his teacher told to him but not write about the reason that why he like environment science . Moreover,he did not write even anything about relationship between his major and activities. As a result of this , he received low marks for his first draft.

So , we can see that , finding a relationship between activities and major is extreme important!!!!!
